Buccaneer Isles Game Demo

(Dev. 2022)

Project Summary:

For my final level design project in my game design course at VCAD, I had to create a short game demo using Unreal Engine. While we were allowed to use Quixel Bridge assets, I decided to challenge myself by making my own custom 3D assets and foliage in a stylized art style. I went with a pirate-themed gameplay concept where you sail around a small island cluster, searching for treasure and blasting obstacles with a cannon—while avoiding a pirate ship that would chase you if you got too close. I’m really proud of the environment and concept I created. I didn’t have as much time to focus on blueprinting all the game mechanics, but even without a fully fleshed-out gameplay system, I was really happy with the final result!
