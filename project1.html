CRASH TEST CAR GAME!!!

(Dev. 2022)

Project Summary:

For my final project in my Python coding course at VCAD, I decided to go a little overboard. The course focused on using Python inside Autodesk Maya, and while most students made simple tools to speed up animation or modeling, I went in a completely different direction—I made a game. My script generated a randomized obstacle course where you control a car dodging obstacles, with everything—from the terrain to the obstacles—being procedurally placed. Every time you restart, the obstacles move to new locations, so there’s no memorizing your way through. I spent over 40 hours on it (way too much, honestly), but the final result was awesome and totally worth it!
