Firebird Security Hangar

(Dev. 2023)

Project Summary:

For my final graduation project at VCAD, I chose to create a Sci-Fi themed environment game concept. I was cautioned against doing a fully original design as designing a custom Sci-fi scene posed higher challenges than simply creating assets that were based off an existing concept. That being said, I did use a combination of star wars ships and a bit of mood inspiration from the fallout games. The story behind the scene was that the player was a new hire into a security position in charge of protecting a nearby asteroid drilling station. THe entire scene was custom modeled and textured using Maya and substance painter, and all final renders remained inside Maya. The original blockout was completed inside UE5, but as the environment was the goal and acheiving the best possible renders I chose to finalize my scene within Maya. I am very proud of the final result and created an HD demo video for this project that can we watched below.
